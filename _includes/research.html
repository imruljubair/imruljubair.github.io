Research

My interests include Data Visualization, Computer Graphics and Computer Vision. In my MSc research, I collaborated with the German Climate Computing Centre (DKRZ) and developed a technique called "Icosahedral Maps" for efficient multiresolution visualization of climate model, i.e. ICON (Icosahedral Nonhydrostatic) model. I have research experience in Image processing and Computer Vision. I worked on Noise Removal, Object Detection and OCR. Currently, I am involved in Deep Learnig based Computer Vision projects [see my curent projects].
