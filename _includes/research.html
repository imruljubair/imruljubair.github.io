Research

My interest includes Computer Graphics and Computer Vision. In my MSc research, I collaborated with the German Climate Computing Centre (DKRZ) and developed a technique called "Icosahedral Maps" for an efficient wavelet-based multiresolution visualization of climate data, i.e. ICON (Icosahedral Nonhydrostatic). I have research experience in Computer Vision, and currently I am involved in Deep Learning based projects [see my curent projects].
