Research

I have interest in Visualization, Graphics and Computer Vision. In my MSc research, I collaborated with the German Climate Computing Centre and developed a technique for efficient multiresolution visualization of climate data. For more details please have a look at my thesis (and slides).


I have research experiences in Image Processing and Computer Vision since my undergrad. I worked on noise remval, book region detection and character recognition.


Some of my selected publications are listed below.

  1. Jubair M. I., Alim U., Röber N., Clyne J. & Mahdavi-Amiri A., “Icosahedral Maps for a Multiresolution Representation of Earth Data”, 21st International Symposium on Vision, Modeling and Visualization, Bayreuth, 2016.
  2. Jubair M. I., Alim U., Röber N., Clyne J., Mahdavi-Amiri A. & Samavati. F., “Multiresolution Visualization of Digital Earth Data via Hexagonal Box Spline Wavelets”, IEEE Scientific Visualization Conference, Chicago, 2015.
  3. Jubair M. I. & Banik P., “A Technique to Detect Books from Library Bookshelf Image”, IEEE 9th International Conference on Computational Cybernetics, Hungary, 2013.
