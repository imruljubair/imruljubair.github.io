Research

I have interest in Visualization, Graphics and Computer Vision. In my MSc research, I collaborated with the German Climate Computing Centre and developed a technique for efficient multiresolution visualization of climate data. For more details please have a look at my thesis (and slides).


I have research experiences in Image Processing and Computer Vision since my undergrad. One of my ongoing projects is - Bangladeshi sign language detection using neural networks. I am mentoring a group of my students to work on this project and to build a dataset called - BdSLimset.
