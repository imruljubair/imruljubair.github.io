Research

I have interest in Visualization, Graphics and Computer Vision. In my MSc research, I collaborated with the German Climate Computing Centre (DKRZ) and developed a technique called "Icosahedral Maps" for efficient multiresolution visualization of climate model, i.e. ICON (Icosahedral Nonhydrostatic) model.


I also have interest in Image Processing and Computer Vision since my undergrad. I worked on noise removal, book region detection and character recognition.
